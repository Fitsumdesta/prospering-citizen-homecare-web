Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

Overview

Prospering Citizen Home Care Services ("we", "us", or "our") operates the PC Homecare mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: Name, email address, and role (caregiver/admin)
  • Precise location: GPS coordinates captured at clock-in and clock-out for Electronic Visit Verification (EVV) compliance required by Virginia DMAS
  • Visit data: Clock-in/out times, visit notes, and care plan task completions
  • Incident reports: Details and location of any incidents reported during visits
  • Messages: Communications between caregivers and administrators within the app

How We Use Your Information

  • To verify caregiver visits as required by Virginia DMAS EVV regulations
  • To manage caregiver schedules, timesheets, and client care plans
  • To facilitate communication between caregivers and agency administrators
  • To generate compliance reports required by state and federal regulations

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Virginia DMAS: EVV location and visit data as required by law
  • Supabase: Our secure cloud database provider (data encrypted at rest and in transit)

Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit using TLS and at rest in our secure database. Access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. To submit a deletion request, visit our Account Deletion page.
