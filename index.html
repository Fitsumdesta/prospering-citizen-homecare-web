Our Story

Built on compassion,
rooted in community.

Prospering Citizen Homecare was founded with a simple but powerful belief: every person deserves to age with dignity, surrounded by familiar comforts and the people they love.

We are a locally owned and operated homecare agency bringing professional, heartfelt care directly into your home. Our caregivers are not just employees — they are dedicated individuals who truly care about the wellbeing of each client.

  • Fully licensed, bonded, and insured caregivers
  • Rigorous background checks and ongoing training
  • Personalized care plans reviewed and updated regularly
  • Direct communication with family members always
  • Rooted in respect for cultural and personal values

Frequently Asked Questions
How do I change my visit schedule?

Call our office at 703-414-9213 or send a message through this portal. Schedule changes require at least 48 hours notice when possible.

How do I request a different caregiver?

Contact our office. We take caregiver compatibility seriously and will work with you to find the best match.

How is my billing calculated?

Billing is based on actual hours of care provided each visit. Invoices are sent monthly. Contact us for billing questions.

What is Electronic Visit Verification (EVV)?

EVV is required by Virginia law. Your caregiver's GPS location is recorded at clock-in and clock-out to verify that care was delivered at your home.
