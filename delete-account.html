Request removal of your account and all associated personal data from the PC Homecare platform.

How to Request Deletion

To request deletion of your account and data, send an email to our support team with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". Please include:

  • Your full name
  • The email address associated with your account
  • A brief confirmation that you want your account deleted
Send Deletion Request

What Data Gets Deleted

Upon processing your request, we will permanently delete:

  • Your account profile (name, email, role)
  • Visit and timesheet records associated with your account
  • GPS clock-in/clock-out location data
  • Messages and incident reports you submitted
Note: Deletion requests are processed within 30 days. Some data may be retained for a limited period where required by Virginia DMAS Electronic Visit Verification regulations.
